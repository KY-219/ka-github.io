OUR ACTIVITIES

LAND BASED ACTIVITIES

Lochquarry is set in the heart of the Argyll hills, surrounded by stunning scenery and a wide range of land-based activities. Visitors can take part in a wide range of land-based activities on and off site.

HILLWALKING

From short walks around the site to Munro-bagging expeditions, Lochquarry has it all! Walks can be tailored to suit any age or experience of groups and can last from one hour to all day adventures.

Max group size 12.

Age 6+.

ARCHERY

Are you the next Robin Hood? Learn to hold a bow and fire an arrow and take part in fast and fun shootout competitions.

Max group size 6.

Age 6+.

ORIENTEERING

Set in the centre's grounds, find all the markets and make it back in time to show off your superior navigation knowledge.

Group of 2+, one map per group.

Age 6+.

AXE THROWING

Take yourself back to a time of Vikings and have a go at throwing an axe. Try to hit the target, better yet throw yourself a bullseye.

Max group size 6.

Age 10+.

OUR ACTIVITIES

WATER BASED ACTIVITIES

Lochquarry is set on the banks of Loch Quarr, a large freshwater loch. Visitors can take part in a wide range of water-based activities Water-based activities all take place on Lochquarry itself.

CANOEING

Work single-handedly or in pairs to canoe the length of Lochquarry. You can even take a picnic with you and explore some of the Loch's island.

Max group size 8 boats (up to 16 people).

Age 6+.

KAYAKING

Have a go at padding, rolling and rafting in one of our brand new kayaks.

Max group size 8.

Age 8+.

PADDLEBOARDING

Take control of one of the Centre's two RIBs out on Lochquarry and try your hand powerboating.

Max group size 6.

Age 8+.

'The Scouts loved every second of it, especially the powerboating' - Martin Bainbridge, Scout Leader

ROPE BASED ACTIVITIES

Lochquarry is set in the heart of the Argyll hills, surrounded by stunning scenery and a wide range of rope-based activities. All rope-based activities take place on site with full safety equipment provided.

CLIMBING

Scale the hights of one of the local quarry slabs.

Max group size 8.

Age 8+.

ABSEILING

Take the scary step and absell from the top of one of the local quarry slabs. There is a lovely view...if you are brave enough to look down!

Max group size 8.

Age 8+.

POLE CLIMB

Even wondered how telephone engineers get to the top of the telephon poles? Well, here's your chance to find out.

Max group size 8.

Age 8+.
