About Lochquarry Outdoor Centre

Lochquarry Outdoor Centre is set in acres of land in the heart of the majestic Argyll hills. On our doorstep is not only magnificent scenery, but also a breath-taking selection of outdoor and adventurous activities.

The centre offers a wide range of activities for all ages and abilities, from canoeing and kayaking to climbing and abseiling. All activities are run under the supervision of qualified instructors and all safety equipment is provided.

Lochquarry Outdoor Centre is the perfect place for schools, youth groups, families and individuals to experience the great outdoors and have fun!

Our customer comment: 'The kids had a ball and did't want to leave' - Mr. Evans, PE teacher.
