Our People

With activity designed to meet the needs of all ages and experiences of young people, Lochquarry truly brings adventure to everyone.

Each activities is run under the instruction of one of our highly trained staff and all safety equipment is provided. Let's meet some of the Lochquarry staff:

Robbie Elliot

Marion Hunter
